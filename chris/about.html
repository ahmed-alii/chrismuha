ABOUT THE OWNER

We have over ten years of experience in the computer repair career.
We have repaired numerous software problems, including but not limited to, correcting settings to satisfy the user, making computers and mobile devices more secure, optimized and as ready as they were as soon as they came out of the box! We also have hardware experience in building you your dream computer, or upgrading your old slow sluggish machine to a computer that can perform tasks, handle complex operations, and more!
The computers we build will last you a lifetime, as there are endless possibilities when you use one of our computers!
Computer slowing down, not a problem, we are here to tune it up to as good as new.
Did you buy a used computer? Bring it to us and let it be tuned to perform like you’ve never seen before! We are committed to quality work, and ensuring your computers and mobile devices are performing at their peak!

The owner, Chris, is disabled with severe anxiety (GAD) and ASD/ADHD, as well as Asthma and COPD. He has many symptoms, including tics, both verbal and motor, and in no way does he intend to be unprofessional and is sorry if he makes you uncomfortable, disturbed, or offended.

We feel remote assistance is not only the most convenient for the customer, but is also a great way to social distance to protect yourselves from any wide-spread illnesses, such as the COVID virus.
We sanitize all work surfaces in-between each customer’s appointment.
We also provide on-site services to those that are willing to bring their computer to us, or transport the owner, Chris, to their location to service it.

The owner, Chris, wrote a book called The Fundamentals of Computer IT! My goal is to help those that do not know how to use a computer learn the basics of computing. In addition to that, Chris also wants to teach those looking to be a computer professional how to do so, by giving my tips, tricks, and repair knowledge. Chris hopes that you will enjoy this book and learn many things from the most in depth information that you will ever read on
The Fundamentals of Computer IT!
