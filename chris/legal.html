  • We do not repair broken screens because of right to repair limitations
    for not only consumers, but for repair technicians who do not have Apple Certification
    (unnecessary).
    Therefore, they do not have access to Apple’s OEM
    (Original Equipment Manufacturer) components,
    which means any other components used, quality or not, have been locked via software.
    The components cannot be unlocked for use in Apple’s devices by anyone but Apple.
    If they are used, even if they are compatible, they simply will not be fully functional.
    Governments all around the world are actively working to change this from a legal standpoint.


  • The first reason we do not repair printers or re-fill ink cartridges is there is a high-risk and inaccessible components.
    That is because such hardware is built to only go together once (soldered).
    There is a high-risk of damaging components during servicing.
    The other reason is because components that are irreplaceable due to unavailability to anyone but the manufacturers’.
    *We CAN change ink cartridges.
