Hello, my name is Chris Muha, I am the Owner and Lead Computer Technician of Muha Computer Repair. I have ten plus years of computer servicing and building experience. I install and maintenance (repair) software for desktop and mobile devices! I provide maintenance for the software (updates, tune-up, etc.) and hardware (replace faulty hardware as needed and upgrades). As for laptops, I upgrade storage (Hard Drive) and memory (RAM) for no matter if it is for tune up or repair!

However, I usually do not go beyond the point of those parts, but it depends. I will not open a laptop beyond points that require significant disassembly, so I would have to see your machine in order to be able to service your upgrade/repair needs that suit your system the best... I do not service hardware for mobile phones and tablets. However, I can diagnose issues and refer you to places that will do it properly and of quality if needed.

I also do software repair and Hard Drive upgrades for not only computers such as desktop and laptops, but also game consoles! I build quality desktop computers to the specifications that the customer needs! I can help you find quality electronics no matter if it is a television, computer, phone, tablet, etc. I can even help troubleshot television software, such as cable boxes and streaming services on smart devices such as smartphones, tablets, and streaming devices such as Roku, Apple TV, Fire TV etcetera!
