Muha Computer Repair is a small computer repair business located in Boonville NY.


We service all of Upstate New York and beyond.
We offer remote services to all of my customers, so you do not have to leave your home or office to get the assistance you need.
In addition to that, you do not have to take time out of your day to come to us. Why? We come to you by way of Virtual Computer Repair.
We can remotely access your computer and show you how to do things and repair many software issues you are having.
We are able to offer you this convenience because most software issues do not require on-site servicing.


We offer convenient and honest high-quality computer services with a flexible schedule.
We have several years of experience in computer diagnostic and repair services. Not only that, we ensure you are secure when using the internet and give you a plan to help protect and prevent your computers and devices from ever having issues again.
We make sure to keep our customers in the loop on what services are being performed, getting consent before repair, and discussing all options available to you.
If there are no software or hardware repair or software update or hardware upgrade service provided, we do not charge our customers.


If you need a new computer, desktop, laptop, or even a new smartphone or tablet, we are here to help you with that too.
We use only the highest-quality components or computer builds and upgrades.
We have helped several customers shop for what computers and devices they have needed and have even built high-quality desktop computers that you cannot get at a retail store.
Plus, our computer builds and hardware upgrades are guaranteed by the manufacturers of the components to be long lasting with no defects or failures.
So, what are you waiting for? Contact us today for all of your tecnological needs. We'll be waiting to assist you with the utmost pleasure.
